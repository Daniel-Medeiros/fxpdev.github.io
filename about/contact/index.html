Freenode IRC

Join us for live chat on Freenode IRC, all members are present in the #freexperia channel, though they might not always be at the computer. If you ask a question but don't get an immediate response just stick around, someone will eventually reply.

Google+ Community

We have a dedicated Google+ community where FXP users help each other. Be sure to drop by and say hi!

XDA Developers

Each device has its own FXP release thread on XDA Developers, the largest online Android community. Most likely your question has already been asked and answered there!
